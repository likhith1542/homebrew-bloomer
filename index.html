Install in seconds
with Homebrew

No App Store. No account. Just paste and press enter.

Step 01
Get Homebrew
If you don't have Homebrew installed yet, paste this in Terminal first.
Step 02
Add the tap
Add Liks' Homebrew tap so your Mac knows where to find Bloomer.
Step 03
Install Bloomer
Install the cask and Bloomer appears in your menu bar instantly.
Terminal — Step 1 (skip if you have Homebrew)
# Install Homebrew (skip if already installed)
$ /bin/bash -c "$(curl -fsSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Homebrew/install/HEAD/install.sh)"
Terminal — Step 2
# Add the Bloomer tap
$ brew tap likhith1542/homebrew-bloomer
Terminal — Step 3
# Install Bloomer
$ brew install --cask bloomer
🌱 Installing Bloomer...
✅ Bloomer is now in your menu bar
Terminal — Update anytime
# Keep Bloomer up to date
$ brew upgrade --cask bloomer
🔒 First launch may show a security prompt — right-click the app → Open to proceed. This is a one-time step.
What makes it special

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

🌿
Lives in menu bar
Bloomer sits quietly at the top of your screen. No windows, no Dock icon — just a tiny swaying plant always within reach.
⏰
Smart reminders
Set daily, weekly, or custom interval reminders. When it's time to care, macOS notifies you with a water drop sound.
🌱
5 growth stages
Your plant starts as a seed and grows through Sprout, Seedling, Plant, and Bloom — each stage a milestone worth celebrating.
🎯
Your targets, your pace
Set exactly how many cares each stage requires. 5 or 500 — you decide how long each phase of growth takes.
💾
Persistent memory
Your plant's progress is saved forever. Restart your Mac — your plant picks up exactly where you left off.
🌙
Dark & light mode
Adapts automatically to your macOS appearance. The plant looks beautiful in both — crisp greens on dark, rich on light.
